EyeSore Lore

Mr. Christopher Spendlebrok was an ordinary person. Or so it seems. You see, Christopher was a wizard with a mission. The gods above are cruelly ruling the universe through wires, connections between a cause of death and an action. When something dies on the cause of death, the action that is connected to the cause of death through a wire is triggered.

One day, the monocle falls from the sky. This is what the gods used to see and create the wires that control the universe. This is where the starting cutscene takes place, in the field outside Christopher's house, which, legend has it is inside of a farming simulator, although this hasn't been confirmed. Christopher exitedly picks up the monocle, as this could be the turning point, the thing that lets him destroy the gods. He puts it over his eye, and, for only a second, sees the wires. Unfortunately the gods know of his sins, and summon his eyeball to pop out and run underground, under which is a large cave system.

Eventually, Christopher enchants a humble ice cream seller, who barges through the ground into the tunnels and starts chasing the eyeball. Unfortunately, in a cruel twist, because Christopher encarnated Ice Cream Kid as a last desperate messure, he is crushed by a cave-in triggered by the Ice Cream Kid's entrance to the caves. In the eyes of the gods, Christopher had commited many a terrible sin, and that was punishment built into the universe that the current gods' infinitely more powerful fathers had created.

Beyond the site of one of the areas affected by the cave-in, using the double jump glitch, you can get to a memorial to Christopher, featuring an unused dark tileset and Christopher's other eye. There is some text which reads:

"In memory of Christopher Spendlebrok, who died fighting the gods. May this be a warning to all who seek to destroy powers they cannot understand. This is a warning that you have already heeded, eyeball. You have been forever trapped underground, along with the monocle. You must never defy the power of the gods."

In a way, Christopher's mission has been achieved. The gods are unable to continue ruling the universe, with no way to create and edit wires. However, there are many wires still in place, even if they are out of the gods' reach which still keep mortals from reaching any sort of power. Christopher's eyeball (who is not controlled by the gods anymore and now knows its mistakes) and the Ice Cream Kid both feel a need to avenge the death of Christopher, and so we might make a sequel to EyeSore, either in our free time or for OGPC (if the theme allows for it).

Anyways, as the credits say, thank you so much for playing EyeSore if you did. This was a real challenge for all of our team and we are very proud of what we have accomplished. If you loved EyeSore and want to thank us, you can subscribe to our youtube channel and watch our videos!
